Community guidelines

We’re here to grow together. Keep it kind, focused, and helpful—so every creator feels safe to learn out loud.

Respect Safety Helpful
  • Be supportive: encourage, don’t shame. Everyone starts somewhere.
  • No spam: share links only when relevant; avoid repeated self-promo.
  • Give value: when asking for feedback, share your goal + context.
  • Protect privacy: don’t share private chats or personal info without consent.
  • Collaborate ethically: clear expectations, credit, and respect for time.
